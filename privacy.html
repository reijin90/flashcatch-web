Privacy Policy

Information Collection

  • FlashCatch! does not collect any personal information without your explicit consent
  • Camera access permissions are optional - you can deny them as they are not required for the app to function
  • All data stays on your device - nothing is stored on external servers

Crash Reporting

  • The app uses Sentry.io for crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Basic device information and network information like IPs may be collected when crashes occur
  • This information is strictly used for improving app stability
  • You can review Sentry's privacy practices at: https://sentry.io/privacy/

Usage of Information

  • Any collected data is solely used to improve app functionality and performance
  • To provide technical support and fix potential issues

Security

  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • Your privacy and security are the top priority of FlashCatch!

Last updated: March 2024

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through the contact link in the app
